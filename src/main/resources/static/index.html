Basic Library Management Platform

You can press on every book you can see - it'll display its details.

  1. Use "Categories" tab to display categories. There you can choose one to filter all the books from chosen category. Entering one of the books will show you its details.
  2. Use "Top Author" tab to display top authors from our books database.
